About Mission

Mobilize community and economic development by working with local interests and outside institutions

History

Puente was founded by former Peace Corps volunteers who wanted to continue making an impact in international development. In the Dominican Republic, there are forgotten communities that don’t have their basic needs and by being bridge between developing communities, development organizations, and data-driven solutions, we can make a difference.

 How Are We Addressing It?

By building customized tech tools for international development! In order to “bridge” the gap between data and development, Puente created a proprietary data collection application as a means to create a reliable database of reliable, quantitative and qualitative data that reflects the development needs of resource-challenged communities.

 Let's Tackle This Together

With your data collection, we're one step closer to empowering community development with modern computational tools!
